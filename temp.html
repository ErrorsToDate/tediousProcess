BooksBeforeBars

HTML/CSS Sodadb

Books Before Bars is an initiative put together by students from Keene High School to collect books for the Chesire Country Correctional Facility. Books are collected from boxes placed around Keene. Then, they are logged and delivered to the Correctional Facility biweekly. The goal of this project is to increase the size and quality of the Correctional Facility's library, to lower recidivism rates, and to raise awareness of rehabilitation in jails and prisons.

https://booksbeforebars.org/
